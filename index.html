Introducing
capacit.

Built in collaboration with Georgia Tech to be launched at the Fall 2020 semester. Stay safe and maintain social distancing through live occupancy data for buildings across campus. Avoid crowds, long lines, and find empty spots for studying or relaxing outside of your dorm.

Protect public health.

As the winner of Georgia Tech and Emory's EHGI Covid-19 Hackathon, Capacit was built with one goal in mind: to protect the health of our community during the time of a pandemic. Capacit gives students and faculty necessary data to make health-conscious decisions and aid the transiton back to campus.

How it works

Beep Boop

We collect our data with wifi signal. We protect user privacy by anonymizing device IDs connected to access points across campus and speaking in really low voices.
